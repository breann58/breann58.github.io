What is a doula?

A doula's role varies widely from birth to birth, but always with the underlying goal of championing the parent's own decisions and experience. For myself this has taken the form of prenatal visits to assist in education and birth planning; prompting additional information from medical staff during labor; helping the partner find helpful ways to assist mom during contractions; being a constant presence during the entire labor.
